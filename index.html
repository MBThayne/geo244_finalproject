Multnomah County Library System
Multnomah County Library is the public library system serving Portland and Multnomah County, Oregon. A continuation of the Library Association of Portland, established in 1864, the system now has 19 branches. It is the largest library system in Oregon, serving a population of 724,680 with more than 425,000 registered borrowers. According to the Public Library Association, it ranks second among U.S. libraries, based on circulation of books, materials, and ranks first among libraries serving fewer than one million residents.
